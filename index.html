Our Classes

All Academy classes start on Sept 5 (Tu), Sept 6 (Wed) and Sept 11 (Mon). There are no breaks during the semester. Classes end on Dec 12 (Tu), Dec 13 (Wed) and Dec 18 (Mon). PSEO classes run in sync with the Academy classes even if it is different than the college on-campus schedule.

The Academy is not a school. To qualify under state guidelines as a home educator, the parent must be the primary teacher and be ultimately responsible for assigning grades and credit for total coursework. Within the Minnesota state guidelines, families must be registered as home educators with their local school district to participate in our classes.
